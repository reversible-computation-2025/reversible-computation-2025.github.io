Registration


The conference fee for early registrations (until June 1st) is 250€, for late registrations it is 350€. The conference fee includes access to the conference, lunches, conference dinner, as well as beverages and snacks during the breaks.
Accompanying persons are welcome as well, their fee is 120€ and includes lunches and conference dinner only.

Please fill in the registration pdf form and send it to

You will receive an invoice with payment instructions within a couple of days.

Recommended hotels:

  • Best Western Plus Steinsgarten (99€ single room incl. breakfast)
  • Hotel am Ludwigsplatz (89€ double room for a single person incl. breakfast)
When booking, please mention that you are attending a university event.
